About the job

At Google , we are dedicated to building an exceptional Product Design team in Vietnam. Our mission is to deliver high-quality designs that create value, move swiftly, and stand out from the crowd. We cherish individuals who exhibit creative hustle, think innovatively, are self-motivated, and persistently achieve results even in the face of seemingly insurmountable challenges. We thrive in a fast-paced environment, continuously pushing boundaries, exploring new possibilities, and embracing failure as an invaluable opportunity for perpetual growth and learning. The Product Design team at One Mount is the perfect place for those who are eager to work with us.

Responsibilities
  • Strong graphic design skills and a keen eye for detail
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Familiarity with design principles and practices
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Knowledge of web design and user experience (UX) principles is a plus
  • Familiarity with Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator or InDesign is a plus
